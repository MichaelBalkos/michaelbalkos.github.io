Graphic Design Application

This Java-based graphic design application was inspired by the well-known online tool, Canva.com. With a user-friendly interface built using JavaFX, this program offers a wide range of features for creating and enhancing graphic designs. You can add and modify elements such as text, circles, rectangles, and images with ease. The program not only offers seamless account management through sign up, login, and user detail modification, but also gives you the ability to export your canvas to your device.

The system was constructed with object-oriented principles such as encapsulation, inheritance, and polymorphism, as well as implementing design patterns like MVC, creational, and singleton. It also strictly follows SOLID design principles.
